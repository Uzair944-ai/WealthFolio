# 📈 WealthFolio

**Free, offline portfolio tracker for the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX).**
Track your holdings, dividends, capital gains, cash flows and tax — privately, on
your device. Live PSX prices and optional cloud backup included.

## ⬇️ Download

**[Download the latest APK »](../../releases/latest)**

1. Open the downloaded `.apk` on your Android phone.
2. If it warns *"unsafe / unknown source"*, tap **More details → Install anyway**.
   (That's Android's standard note for any app not installed from the Play Store —
   it doesn't mean the app is harmful.)
3. Open **WealthFolio** and start adding your trades.

## ✨ Features
- 📊 Dashboard with total return, XIRR, market value and P/L
- 💼 Holdings (current & closed) with **live PSX prices** + manual override
- 💰 Dividends with tax & zakat, and a searchable period picker
- 🏦 Cash flows with "available to invest"
- 🔒 Optional PIN app-lock, light/dark themes
- ☁️ Optional cloud backup & sync
- 📴 Works **fully offline** — your data stays on your device

## 🔐 Privacy
No ads, no trackers, no analytics. Your portfolio never leaves your device unless
you choose to enable Cloud Sync. See the in-app Privacy Policy.
